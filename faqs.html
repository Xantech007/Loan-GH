Any Ghanaian aged 21–60 with a valid Ghana Card, regular income (salary or business), and an active mobile money account or bank account can apply.

• Valid Ghana Card
• Recent payslip or proof of income
• Bank statement or MoMo statement (last 3–6 months)
• One passport-sized photo

Once all required documents are submitted, approval usually takes 24–48 hours. You will receive an SMS and email notification.

Approved loans are sent directly to your Mobile Money wallet or bank account within minutes of final approval.
