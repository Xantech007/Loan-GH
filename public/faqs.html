Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about CedisPay loans.

Who is eligible to apply for a CedisPay loan?

Anyone who is 18 years or older, has a valid Ghana Card, a steady income (payslip or proof of income), and an active mobile money account or bank account can apply.

How much can I borrow?

You can borrow from GHS 500 up to GHS 50,000 depending on your income, credit history, and repayment capacity.

What documents do I need to apply?

• Valid Ghana Card
• Recent payslip or proof of income
• Bank statement or Mobile Money statement (last 3–6 months)
• Recent passport-sized photo

How long does approval take?

Once all required documents are submitted, approval usually takes 24–48 hours.

How do I receive the money?

Approved loans are disbursed directly to your Mobile Money wallet (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo) or bank account within minutes.

What are the repayment terms?

You can choose flexible repayment periods from 3 to 24 months. You’ll pay fixed monthly installments via mobile money or direct debit.

Are there any hidden charges?

No. We are 100% transparent. All fees and interest rates are clearly shown before you accept the loan.

Can I repay early?

Yes! You can repay your loan early at any time with no penalty or extra charges.

What happens if I miss a payment?

We understand things happen. Contact us immediately if you foresee any difficulty. Late payments may attract a small penalty and affect future loan eligibility.

How do I contact customer support?

Call us on +233 50 123 4567 or email support@cedispay.com. We’re available Monday–Saturday, 8 AM–6 PM.
